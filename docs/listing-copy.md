## Detailed description

**Your AI growth team, inside Shopify.**

ShopHero turns plain-English requests into real changes in your store. Ask it to
rewrite product descriptions, redesign a product page, fix store speed, improve
on-page SEO, plan and write blog content, or build trust sections — and ShopHero
does the work, then stages it for your review before anything goes live.

Every change is made on a safe, unpublished copy of your theme — your live store
is never touched until you click Approve & Publish. You get a visual preview and a
diff of every change, full version history, and one-click rollback, so you stay in
control at all times.

ShopHero also continuously scores your store's health — SEO, content, catalog,
speed, and AI visibility — and gives you a ranked, one-tap list of the highest-
impact fixes. Its AI Visibility (AEO) tools help structure your store so AI
shopping assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity can read and
recommend it.

**What you can do**
- Edit and build your storefront by describing what you want, in plain English
- One-click optimizations for SEO, conversion, speed, and content
- A store-health scorecard that ranks the next best fix by impact
- AI Visibility (AEO): make your store readable and recommendable by AI assistants
- Generate product descriptions, pages, FAQs, and blog articles
- A daily content plan you approve and publish
- Brand Kit + long-term memory so output stays on-brand
- Approval-first workflow with version history and one-click rollback

**Safe by design**
ShopHero works through Shopify's official APIs with least-privilege permissions,
edits an unpublished working copy of your theme, and never publishes anything
without your explicit approval. It does not access orders or customer data.
